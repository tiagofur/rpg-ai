# Privacy Policy - RPG-AI Supreme

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to RPG-AI Supreme ("the App", "we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and
protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using RPG-AI Supreme, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this
policy, please do not use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Email address, username, and password when you create an account.
- **Profile Information:** Character name, avatar, and game preferences.
- **Communications:** Messages you send through the in-game chat system or when contacting support.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the app, including game actions, progress, achievements, and
  playtime.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and regional settings.
- **Connection Data:** IP address, connection type, and time zone.
- **Performance Data:** Technical information about errors, load times, and app performance.

### 2.3 Payment Information

If you make in-app purchases, payments are processed by third parties (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or Stripe).
We do not store complete credit card information. We only receive transaction confirmations and basic subscription data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide the Service:** Create and maintain your account, process your game progress, and generate personalized
  content through artificial intelligence.
- **Improve Experience:** Personalize game narrative, adjust difficulty, and recommend content based on your
  preferences.
- **Communications:** Send you game notifications, updates, and respond to your inquiries.
- **Security:** Detect fraud, abuse, and protect game integrity.
- **Analytics:** Understand how users interact with the app to improve our services.
- **Legal Obligations:** Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

## 4. Use of Artificial Intelligence

RPG-AI Supreme uses third-party artificial intelligence services (Google Gemini, OpenAI) to generate personalized
narrative content. When you play:

- Your actions and text inputs are processed by AI services to generate narrative responses.
- We do not share your personally identifiable information with AI services; only the necessary game context.
- AI-generated responses are based on game context and your actions, not on external personal profiles.

## 5. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

### 5.1 Service Providers

- **Cloud Infrastructure:** To securely store data.
- **AI Services:** Google Cloud AI, OpenAI for content generation.
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe, Apple, Google for transactions.
- **Analytics Services:** To improve the app (anonymized data).

### 5.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

### 5.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

## 6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.
- Secure password storage with hashing.
- Restricted access to personal data.
- Regular security monitoring.

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services. Game session data may
be retained for historical analysis. If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal
information within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information.
- **Correction:** Correct inaccurate information.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and personal data.
- **Portability:** Receive your data in structured format.
- **Objection:** Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for consent-based processing.

To exercise these rights, contact: **privacy@rpgai.app**

## 9. Children's Privacy

RPG-AI Supreme is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).
We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we
will delete it.

## 10. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure transfers
comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use local storage technologies to:

- Keep you logged in.
- Remember your preferences.
- Improve app performance.

We do not use third-party advertising tracking cookies.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

- **Email:** privacy@rpgai.app
- **Support:** support@rpgai.app
